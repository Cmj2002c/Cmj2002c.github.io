Fun Facts

  • The Badgers have appeared in the NCAA tournament 26 times, including a streak of 19 in a row
  • The Badgers won the National Championship in 1941
  • Wisconsin has made it to the NCAA Final Four 4 times in its history
  • The basketball team also plays at the Kohl Center, where crews convert the arena from ice to court
  • Wisconsin Basketball has had 43 players taken in the NBA Draft all time
