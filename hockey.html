Background

The Wisconsin Men's Hockey Team have been playing at the Kohl Center since 1998. Before that, they played at the Dane County Coliseum from 1967 to 1998. The men's hockey team has won the National Championship 6 times in their history. They have made the Frozen Four 12 times. The team is currently coached by Mike Hastings who is in his first year with the Badgers. 25 former players have been part of Olympic teams. All-time, 177 Wisconsin men's hockey players have been chosen in the NHL draft.
